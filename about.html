About us

Founded in Jiangmen Guangdong province 2016, a major industrial area in south China, cover an area of 20,000 square meters, DPD is a diversified manufacturer provide a semi-finished and finished foam to automobile manufacturing, electronics industry, health&care and other industries, meanwhile involving mattress foam, latex and related furnitures industry. We had signed a strategic cooperation agreement with Bayer, Basfse and Shell for further development. We invested 10millons RMB for the world leading foam production & testing equipments and 20 worker as start, a anual production approximated to 60millons RMB. Redevelop branding value through the secondary processing of foam, to the Gonghe as the production base constitutes a trans-regional, to serve a number of industries foam production network. Integrity, Value, Environmental is our business concept since start, we chase the endless development and excellence quality, providing the most satisfied service to our clients with eceptional products.
